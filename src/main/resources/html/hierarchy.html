Document Title (H1) - Used once as main document heading

Section Level (H2) - Logically follows H1 in hierarchy

Regular paragraph text - Always contained within sectioning elements

Subsection (H3) - Must follow H2 without skipping levels

Another paragraph - Maintains proper nesting under its heading

Sub-subsection (H4) - Shows continued logical depth

Content under H4 - All headings lead somewhere meaningful

Second H4 - Same level allowed under same H3

More content - Keeping same hierarchy level is fine

Second Main Section (H2) - Another logical H2 after content

Never use headings just for bold text - they must structure content

Footer content - Should not contain headings as it's not a section
